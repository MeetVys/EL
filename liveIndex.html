ABOUT US



E-Summit is one of the biggest Entrepreneurship Conclaves in India. This is our attempt to inspire and educate students, who are inclined towards entrepreneurship,and early entrepreneurs to take and manage bigger risks.We also endeavor to introduce the business-world to students, who are new to the field. Our event will be centered on the theme "A Pragmatic Advent". Via a series of talks, panel discussions, competitions, and other networking events. With the pragmatic advent, as our theme, we aim to answer the most pressing questions among students and young entrepreneurs currently, after the strike of a global pandemic.
